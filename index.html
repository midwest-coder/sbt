Our Story

Humble Beginnings

  • It Begins In Laos

    Born in Laos, I grew up surrounded by the Buddhist culture and looking at all the beautiful statues my country had.

  • Coming to America

    I was able to move to the United States and explore all the wonders it had to offer. This would be an experience like I've never had before.

  • The Stuggle is Real

    After living here for a while I began to enjoy seeing the buddha statues around the country and it made me miss my home and culture and being apart of it.

  • A Light At the End

    I decided it's time to rekindle that relationship with my culture by helping to bring it to the States. Whenever someone wants a Statue I want to help them in finding one that reminds them of home.

  • Now
    Here
    We Are!
